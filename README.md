# PlanMyMeals

**PlanMyMeals** is a mobile app built with **Flutter** and **Dart**, currently under active development. The app is designed to help you plan your nutrition in a simple, personalized, and fully offline way.

---

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| User Profiles | Create personalized profiles including **dietary preferences**, **body type**, **gender**, **weight**, **height**, **activity level**, and more. |
| Calorie & Nutrient Calculations | Calculate your **daily caloric requirements** and macro/micronutrient needs based on **WHO standards** and your personal goals. |
| Personalized Meal Planning | Generate meal plans tailored to your **calories, macronutrient targets, meal preferences, and eating windows**. |
| Recipe Integration | Choose from a local database of recipes or add your **own custom recipes**. Meal plans automatically include recipes that fit your nutritional targets. |
| Fully Offline | All your data stays **on your device**â€”PlanMyMeals does not upload or share your personal information. |

---

## Planned Features

- Flexible meal frequencies and schedules (e.g., intermittent fasting, 2 meals per day, skipping breakfast).  
- Ability to mark favorite recipes and track meal preferences over time.  
- Weekly meal plan overview.  
- Shopping list generation based on planned meals (future version).  

---

## Why PlanMyMeals?

- **Privacy First:** Your data never leaves your device.  
- **Personalized:** Tailored to your body, goals, and dietary needs.  
- **Offline-Friendly:** Works anywhere, no internet connection required.  
- **Flexible & Customizable:** Supports adding your own recipes, dietary preferences, and meal plans.  

---

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** Flutter & Dart  
- **Data Storage:** Local storage using **SQLite**
- **Platform:** iOS & Android (planned)
